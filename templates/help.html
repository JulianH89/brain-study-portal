Help & Frequently Asked Questions

How to Use the Portal

Welcome to the Brain Study Portal! This site is designed to help researchers and students explore single-cell studies related to Alzheimer's Disease.

  • Home Page: Provides a general introduction to the portal.
  • Studies Page: This is the core of the portal. It lists all the publications we have cataloged. You can use the filter controls at the top of the page to narrow down the results by species (e.g., Human, Mouse) or by the specific brain region studied.
  • Help Page: You are here! This page provides information and answers to common questions.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Where does the data come from?

All publication and dataset information is manually curated from public sources like PubMed, Nature, and Science. Each dataset has a direct link to its source where the data can be accessed.

How is the data structured?

We use a relational database to link publications to their corresponding datasets. Each dataset is tagged with the species and the brain region(s) studied to enable effective filtering.

How can I contribute a study to the portal?

Currently, data is added by our internal team. We are planning to add a contribution feature in the future. Please check back later for updates!

Contact Us

Have questions or feedback? Please reach out to our team at contact@brainstudyportal.org.
